Short Bio

My name is Farchan Hakim Raswa. I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science and Information Engineering (CSIE) at National Central University (NCU), Taiwan. I am co-advised by Prof. Jia-Ching Wang and Prof. Chun-Shien Lu.

My research interests lie in the intersection of Deep Learning and Computer Vision, but are not limited to them, with a recent focus on Federated Learning, Medical Image Analysis, and Robust Biometric Security.

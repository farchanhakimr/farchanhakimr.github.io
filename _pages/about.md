### Short Bio

My name is Farchan Hakim Raswa. I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering (CSIE) at National Central University (NCU), Taiwan, co-advised by Prof. Jia-Ching Wang at the DLMSL Lab and Prof. Chun-Shien Lu at Academia Sinica.

My field interests are deep learning, computer vision, and trustworthy AI. Over the past 7 years, I have been working on various topics in computer vision, histopathology images, federated learning, privacy, and trustworthy AI.
